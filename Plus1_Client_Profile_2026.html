🗂️ What This Document Is

The Client Profile is the single source of truth for every client relationship at Plus 1 Technology. It captures everything your team needs to support, manage, and grow an account — from contact info and IT infrastructure to strategic goals, active projects, escalation paths, and decisions made over time. This is an internal document and should not be shared with clients directly.

📝 How to Fill This In
1Start with Overview — company name, status, tier, contract dates, and location. This populates the hero bar above.
2Go to Resources & Docs immediately — paste in all known links so nothing gets lost. This is the most-referenced tab by the team.
3Fill People & Contacts and RACI Matrix — critical for onboarding new team members and managing escalations correctly.
4Complete IT Environment and Tools & Systems — required for any support, project, or procurement work.
5Fill Services & SLA — check the boxes for active services and confirm the SLA tiers with the contract.
6Keep Risk Register, Decisions Log, and Active Projects updated after every QBR, major change, or project milestone.
ℹ️ Living Document: This profile should be reviewed and updated quarterly at minimum — ideally after every QBR. Stale client profiles cause onboarding friction, missed escalations, and support gaps.
