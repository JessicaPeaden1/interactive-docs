Estimate Overview

All 13 options organized by category. Time ranges represent estimated build hours from scoping through delivery — not calendar time. See the Assumptions tab for what's included and excluded.

How to read these estimates: Hours shown are for build and delivery by Jessica & Marc — not Yulia's time. Low end = scoped-down version or when dependencies are already confirmed. High end = full implementation with testing, documentation, and handoff. Estimates do not include ongoing maintenance or iteration after delivery.

What's Always Included

  • Initial scoping / design for each option
  • Prompt writing and iteration
  • Testing against real or representative HOCK content
  • Formatting and delivery of final deliverable
  • Written usage instructions or SOP for Yulia / team
  • One round of revisions based on Yulia feedback

What's Never Included

  • Yulia's time to operate, review, or approve outputs
  • Ongoing content writing or execution after system is live
  • Maintenance or bug fixes post-delivery (scoped separately)
  • Website publishing, embedding, or dev work on HOCK site
  • Account setup or billing for third-party tools (11 Labs, Circle API, etc.)
  • Additional rounds of revision beyond the first
Recommended sequence for scoping: Start with Options 3 and 1 this week (fewest hours, immediate value). Scope Option 5 immediately after — it's the highest-leverage medium build and feeds Options 6, 8, and 10. Full Systems options should only be scoped after Quick Wins and Medium Builds are delivering value.
